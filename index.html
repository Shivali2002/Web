What We Do

To us, each garment has it's own unique story. It is treated with extensive care and crafted to perfection. We work closely with all our clients to meet each need and provide the perfect fit. We guarentee the highest quality in order to help brands build loyalty and reputation.



How We Do It


- Su Misura Manufacturing is a high end source of high fashion garments
- Providing a complete facility of cut to pack high end woven and knit garments
- We conduct all our manufacturing processes with complete business ethics
- All staff is well versed with international standards
- The backbone of our business is our technical team, such as our pattern masters, tailors, QCs, etc. have atleast 25 years of experience
- We are well equipped with cutting facilities and a clothing construction department. Includes single thread, flat and overlock machine
- Our finishing department includes a steam press with broiler with a complete section of racks to provide specialized pressing and check of garments
- We have a specialized packaging department, in order to fulfil complicated or fancy tagging requirements
- We can accept a capacity of 10,000-20,000 high end garments with detailing and 40,000 garments for normal pieces
